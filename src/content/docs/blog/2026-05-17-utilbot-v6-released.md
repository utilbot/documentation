After long development and testing, we are excited to announce the release of Utilbot v6! This major update brings a host of new features, improvements, and optimizations to enhance your experience with Utilbot.

Besides our new Website, we have also made significant changes to the bot itself.

We have updated to discord.js v15 (even if it is still in development), we are also working on major improvements for the backend / database to finally bring even more new features.

In the near future we are rolling out the "Tags" feature, more on that later...
